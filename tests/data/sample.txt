The rain fell softly over the village as Mira walked home from the market. She carried a basket of
apples, bread, and tea leaves. Every evening, Mira stopped by the old bridge to watch the river
flow beneath the fading light. The river was calm tonight, calm and quiet, unlike the busy market
she had left behind. A small dog followed Mira down the road, hoping for bread. Mira smiled and
shared a piece. The dog wagged its tail happily and continued beside her. By the time Mira reached
home, the rain had stopped, and the village was silent once more.
